About Me

Hi, I’m Cristiano! I'm a UX design student who loves fixing small problems that somehow turn into big design ideas. I’m interested in socially responsible design, accessibility, and anything that helps people feel seen and supported. Maker Lab I taught me that you learn a lot by actually building things and by accidentally breaking them first. I approach design with curiosity, empathy, and a habit of asking “why was it made like this?” way too often.
