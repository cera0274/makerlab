About Me

I’m Cristiano Cerasoli, a UX design student interested in socially responsible design and the ways small, practical interventions can create meaningful impact. I’m drawn to human-centered projects that highlight dignity, accessibility, and emotional well-being, especially for people whose needs are overlooked. In Maker Lab I, I learned how hands-on making and iteration can deepen the design process. My design approach blends empathy, curiosity, a commitment to challenging assumptions, and a passion for finding design opportunities hidden within everyday problems.
